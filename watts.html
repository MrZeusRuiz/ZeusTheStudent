“The free man walks straight ahead; he has no hesitations and never looks behind, for he knows that there is nothing in the future and nothing in the past that can shake his freedom.
Freedom does not belong to him; it is no more his property than the wind, and as he does not possess it he is not possessed by it.
And because he never looks behind his actions are said to leave no trace, like the passage of a bird through air.”

― Alan W. Watts
